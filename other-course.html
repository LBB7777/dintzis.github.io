User Experience Design | Dr. Lynn Boyden | Fall 2022

With a group of other students, I worked with JPL Nasa's Cartography and Imaging Node. We tested their site among different user groups, then analyzed these interviews to compile a report making recommendations for changes to their site in order to enhance user understanding, usability, and ease of access, which we then presented to them.
