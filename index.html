I am a graduate student of information studies at the University of California, Los Angeles. My studies focus on archives, museums, and special collections, and how digital technology is used in these spaces. I like to draw in my free time and sometimes I'll make silly little sketches for this site. I'm also active on r/whatsthisbug and always want to know something new about the arthropods around us.
